Features

Everything a reseller needs

Built by resellers, for resellers. Every feature earns its place.

🔎

Scout Mode

Scan any item at a thrift store to instantly see resale value, recent sold prices, and open bounties on ThriftWish.

🌟

ThriftWish

Our reverse marketplace. Buyers post what they want, you find it and get paid. Like bounty hunting for thrifters.

🔄

Auto-Delist

When an item sells on one platform, it automatically delists from the others. No more overselling headaches.

🎨

AI Background Removal

Clean, studio-quality product photos in one tap. No lightbox needed, no editing apps required.

📦

Built-in Shipping

Compare carrier rates, generate labels, and track packages without leaving the app. Powered by Shippo.

🔒

Secure Payments

Stripe Connect escrow protects every ThriftWish transaction. Funds are held until delivery is confirmed.
