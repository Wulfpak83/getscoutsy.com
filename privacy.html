Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026 · Last updated March 15, 2026

MSCLTHRU Unlimited ("MSCLTHRU," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Scoutsy mobile application ("Scoutsy," the "App") and the website located at getscoutsy.com (the "Website"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App and the Website (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways: directly from you, automatically when you use the Service, and from third-party services you connect to your account.

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo. You may register using email and password, Apple Sign-In, or Google Sign-In.
  • Profile Information. You may provide additional information to your profile, including a display name, bio, and profile picture.
  • Listing Content. When you create product listings, we collect the photos you take or upload, product descriptions, pricing information, condition details, sizing, and category selections.
  • ThriftWish Content. When you create a Wish (a request for a specific item), we collect your wish title, inspiration photos, budget range, category, brand, size, color, condition preferences, search radius, and urgency level.
  • Payment Information. When you set up payouts or make purchases through ThriftWish, payment processing is handled by Stripe Connect. We do not store your full credit card number, debit card number, or bank account number on our servers. Stripe collects and processes this information directly in accordance with Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Shipping Information. When you ship items through the Service, we collect shipping addresses, package dimensions, and weight information. Shipping labels are generated through Shippo.
  • Chat Messages. When you communicate with other users through the in-app messaging system, your messages are processed and stored by Stream Chat. We may access message content for safety moderation purposes, including automated filtering of personal contact information and off-platform payment solicitations.
  • Support Communications. If you contact us for customer support, we collect the information you provide in your correspondence, including your name, email address, and the content of your messages.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information. We collect information about the device you use to access the Service, including device model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Photo Provenance Data. When you take photos within the App, we record the timestamp and device identifier associated with each photo. This information is used to establish proof of original creation for DMCA compliance and fraud prevention.
  • Usage Data. We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including the features you use, the actions you take, the time and duration of your activities, and diagnostic data.
  • Log Data. Our servers automatically record information when you access the Service, including your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and timestamps.

1.3 Information Collected With Your Permission

  • Location Data. With your explicit permission, we collect your device's geographic location. Location data is used to show you nearby ThriftWish wishes, suggest safe meetup locations, and calculate distances for local transactions. You may revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. The Service will continue to function with reduced functionality if location access is denied.
  • Camera Access. With your permission, we access your device camera to allow you to take product photos for listings and Scout Mode scans. Camera access is required for core listing functionality.
  • Photo Library Access. With your permission, we access your device's photo library to allow you to upload inspiration photos for ThriftWish wishes and supplementary listing photos.
  • Push Notifications. With your permission, we send push notifications for new messages, order updates, ThriftWish match alerts, and other Service-related communications. You may disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

1.4 Information From Third-Party Platform Connections

When you connect external selling platforms to your Scoutsy account for cross-listing purposes, we collect and store OAuth authentication tokens for those platforms. The platforms you may connect include:

  • Auto-Post Platforms (API integration): eBay, Etsy, TikTok Shop, Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Whatnot.
  • Assisted Platforms (copy-paste workflow): Poshmark, Mercari, Depop, Facebook Marketplace, and OfferUp.

For Auto-Post platforms, we store encrypted OAuth tokens in our database to post listings on your behalf. We do not store your login credentials (username and password) for any connected platform. You may disconnect any platform at any time through the App settings, which will revoke our access and delete the stored tokens.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 Providing and Maintaining the Service

  • Creating and managing your account.
  • Generating and cross-posting product listings to connected platforms.
  • Facilitating ThriftWish transactions between buyers and sellers.
  • Processing payments and managing escrow through Stripe Connect.
  • Generating shipping labels through Shippo.
  • Enabling in-app messaging between buyers and sellers.
  • Managing your subscription through RevenueCat.

2.2 AI-Powered Features

  • Listing Generation. Product photos you take in the App are sent to Google Gemini AI to automatically generate listing titles, descriptions, and category suggestions.
  • Background Removal. Product photos may be processed by Google Gemini AI to remove backgrounds for cleaner listing images.
  • Price Estimation. Product photos and identifying information are processed by Google Gemini AI and eBay's completed listings data to provide resale price estimates.
  • Scout Mode. Photos taken in Scout Mode are analyzed by Google Vision API and Google Gemini AI to identify items, estimate resale value, and match against open ThriftWish wishes.
  • Photo Similarity Matching. When a hunter proposes a ThriftWish match, AI compares the hunter's photos against the buyer's inspiration photos to generate a similarity score.

We process your photos through AI services solely to provide the features described above. We do not use your photos to train AI models. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about how Google processes data sent to its AI services.

2.3 Safety and Moderation

  • Automated filtering of chat messages to prevent sharing of personal contact information, off-platform payment links, and external marketplace links.
  • Photo provenance recording (timestamp and device ID) for DMCA compliance and fraud prevention.
  • Monitoring for counterfeit items and prohibited goods.
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and Prohibited Items Policy.

2.4 Communication

  • Sending transactional notifications (order updates, shipping confirmations, payment receipts, dispute notifications).
  • Sending Service-related announcements and updates.
  • Responding to your customer support inquiries.

2.5 Improvement and Analytics

  • Understanding how users interact with the Service to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Diagnosing technical issues and bugs.
  • Analyzing aggregate usage trends.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on servers operated by Supabase, which uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure located in the United States. OAuth tokens for connected platforms are stored in encrypted form in our Supabase database.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of OAuth tokens at rest.
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies on all database tables to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Rate limiting on all API endpoints.
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Authentication throttling (cooldown and lockout mechanisms) to prevent brute-force attacks.
  • AI kill switch capability to immediately disable all AI processing in the event of a security incident.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Listing content and photos are retained for the duration of your account, plus a reasonable period after deletion for legal and dispute resolution purposes.
  • Transaction records are retained for a minimum of seven (7) years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Chat messages are retained in accordance with Stream Chat's retention policies.
  • Scout Mode scan history is retained for the duration of your account.
  • Photo provenance records (timestamps, device IDs) are retained permanently for DMCA compliance purposes.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within thirty (30) days, except for data we are required to retain by law or for legitimate business purposes as described above.

5.4 Push Notifications

You may enable or disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

5.5 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at legal@getscoutsy.com or through the account settings in the App. Upon receiving your request, we will delete your account and personal information within thirty (30) days, subject to the retention requirements described in Section 4.3.

11. In-App Messaging and Moderation

Messages sent through the in-app messaging system are processed by Stream Chat and are subject to automated moderation. Our moderation system automatically filters messages that contain:

  • Phone numbers or email addresses.
  • Links to off-platform payment services (such as Venmo, CashApp, PayPal, or Zelle).
  • Links to external marketplaces.

This filtering is performed to protect users from fraud and to keep transactions within the safety of the Scoutsy escrow system. Filtered messages are not delivered to the recipient. We may review flagged messages to improve our moderation systems and investigate potential fraud.

12. AI Processing Disclosure

Certain features of the Service use artificial intelligence to process your data:

  • Listing Generation: Photos you take are sent to Google Gemini AI, which analyzes the images and generates suggested titles, descriptions, and pricing. The generated content is presented to you for review and editing before publication.
  • Background Removal: Product photos may be sent to Google Gemini AI to produce images with transparent or clean backgrounds.
  • Scout Mode: Photos taken in Scout Mode are sent to Google Vision API for item identification and to Google Gemini AI for price estimation and wish matching.
  • Photo Similarity: When matching ThriftWish proposals, AI compares photos to generate a similarity score.

Your photos are transmitted to Google's servers for processing and are subject to Google's data handling practices. We do not use your content to train our own AI models. The AI-generated content (titles, descriptions, price estimates) is derived from your photos and publicly available market data.

You may disable AI features by not using the Scout Mode or AI listing generation features. Core listing functionality (manual entry and cross-posting) does not require AI processing.
