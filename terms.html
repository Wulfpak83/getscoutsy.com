2. Description of Service

2.1 Platform Purpose

Scoutsy is a software platform only. Scoutsy provides AI-powered tools for cross-listing resale items across multiple third-party marketplaces and operates a peer-to-peer marketplace called ThriftWish. Scoutsy does not manufacture, store, inspect, handle, or ship any inventory. Scoutsy is not a party to any transaction between buyers and sellers.

2.2 Core Features

  • AI Listing Generation: Photograph an item and receive AI-generated listing titles, descriptions, pricing guidance, and condition assessments.
  • Cross-Posting: Post listings to up to twelve (12) third-party platforms from within the App, including seven (7) Auto-Post platforms with direct API integration and five (5) Assisted platforms with formatted copy-paste output.
  • ThriftWish Reverse Marketplace: A peer-to-peer marketplace where buyers post "wishes" (bounties) for items they want, and hunters (sellers/thrifters) find and propose matching items.
  • Scout Mode: An AI-powered scanning tool that identifies items, provides resale price estimates from historical sales data, and matches items to open ThriftWish bounties.
  • Escrow and Payment Processing: Stripe Connect-based payment facilitation for ThriftWish transactions.
  • Shipping Label Generation: Shipping labels generated through Shippo for ThriftWish transactions.

2.3 Auto-Post Platforms (API Integration)

The following platforms support direct listing via API: eBay, Etsy, TikTok Shop, Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Whatnot (Buy It Now listings only; no live auction support).

2.4 Assisted Platforms (Copy-Paste)

The following platforms are supported through formatted text, cleaned photos, and step-by-step posting checklists: Poshmark, Mercari, Depop, Facebook Marketplace, and OfferUp. Scoutsy does not access or control your accounts on these platforms.

2.5 No Scoutsy Fee on Cross-Posting

Cross-posting listings to any external platform (whether Auto-Post or Assisted) carries zero Scoutsy transaction fees. External platforms may charge their own seller fees, which are governed by those platforms' terms and are not within Scoutsy's control.

7. Buyer Protection Policy

This Buyer Protection Policy governs purchases made through the ThriftWish marketplace within the Scoutsy App. This policy does not apply to transactions on external platforms (see Section 5.2).

7.1 Escrow Protection

All ThriftWish purchases are protected by Stripe Connect escrow. When you pay for an item, your funds are held by Stripe and are not released to the seller until delivery is confirmed and the dispute window has closed without an active dispute.

7.2 The 48-Hour Dispute Window

After a carrier confirms delivery (or after both parties complete a mutual QR check-in for local meetup transactions), a 48-hour dispute window begins. During this window, you may file a dispute if the item is not as described.

To file a dispute, you must:

  • (a) Submit the dispute through the Scoutsy App within 48 hours of confirmed delivery.
  • (b) Provide photographic evidence clearly showing the discrepancy.
  • (c) Provide a written description of the issue.

If no dispute is filed within 48 hours of confirmed delivery, the transaction is considered complete, and funds are automatically released to the seller. This release is final and cannot be reversed by Scoutsy.

7.3 Item Not Received (INR) Claims

  • (a) Tracking Required. Every ThriftWish shipment must include tracking.
  • (b) Tracking Shows Delivered. If tracking confirms delivery but you did not receive the item, your recourse is with the shipping carrier or your local postal service.
  • (c) Tracking Does Not Show Delivery. If the seller cannot provide valid tracking showing delivery, Scoutsy will release escrowed funds back to the buyer.
  • (d) Signature Confirmation. Signature confirmation is automatically required on items with a sale price exceeding $75.00.

7.4 "Not as Described" Disputes

Possible outcomes:

  • Full refund to buyer if the item is materially different from the listing.
  • Partial refund if the discrepancy is minor and both parties agree.
  • No refund (dispute denied) if the item reasonably matches the listing description.

Scoutsy's determination on disputes is final and binding within the ThriftWish marketplace, subject to the arbitration provisions in Section 21.

7.5 Dispute Resolution Timeline

Scoutsy aims to resolve all disputes within seven (7) calendar days. During the dispute period, funds remain in escrow.

7.6 Limitations of Buyer Protection

Buyer protection does not apply to:

  • Transactions on external platforms.
  • Items where the 48-hour dispute window has expired.
  • Buyer's remorse, change of mind, or subjective dissatisfaction.
  • Normal variations in color, texture, or appearance.
  • Items damaged by the buyer after receipt.
  • Disputes filed after the 48-hour window has closed.

8. ThriftWish Reverse Marketplace

8.1 How ThriftWish Works

ThriftWish is a reverse marketplace where buyers post wishes (bounties) describing items they want, including inspiration photos, budget ranges, preferred sizes, conditions, and geographic radius. Hunters browse active wishes and propose matches by photographing items they find.

8.2 Wish Creation

Buyers may create wishes specifying desired items. A wish is not an obligation to purchase. Buyers may decline any proposed match at their sole discretion.

8.3 Match Proposals

Hunters may submit match proposals by providing photos of found items, a proposed price, and any relevant details. Submitting a match proposal does not guarantee a sale.

8.4 AI Match Scoring

Scoutsy uses artificial intelligence to generate a similarity score between wish inspiration photos and hunter-submitted item photos. This score is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute a guarantee of item accuracy, authenticity, condition, or suitability.

8.5 Transaction Completion

When a buyer accepts a match proposal (or a counter-offer is accepted), a binding transaction is created. The buyer is charged the sale price plus applicable fees and shipping costs.

8.6 Scout Mode

Scout Mode allows users to photograph items and receive AI-generated identification, estimated resale values based on historical sales data, and matches to open ThriftWish bounties. Price estimates are for informational guidance only. Scoutsy does not guarantee the accuracy of any price estimate.
